Privacy Policy for Discord Utility | Universe

Effective Date: March 6, 2025

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Discord Utility | Universe, a Chrome extension developed by Universe Utilities Team. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency.

1. Information We Collect

Discord Utility | Universe does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension:

  • Accesses Discord URLs (e.g., `https://discord.com/*`) solely to open minimal chat popups based on the active tab.
  • Uses Chrome's local storage to save user-defined bookmarks for Discord channels, which is stored locally on your device and not shared with us or third parties.

No personal data (e.g., names, emails, or messages) is gathered or processed by this extension.
